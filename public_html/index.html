Quandale Dingle is the name of a Pennsauken high school
football player featured in a series of goofy ahh shitpost memes based on a viral screenshot of a PC login screen for a man with the name.

In late 2021 throughout early 2022 people began using distorted images of people, mostly Black men, to represent Quandale Dingle in ironic memes. The name also inspired a trend where TikTokers create parody RapTV posts about Quandale.
